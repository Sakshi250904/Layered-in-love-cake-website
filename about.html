    Desserts made with love

    Our small and cozy confectionery was created in 2010.We make confectionery products of any complexity exclusively from natural ingredients.
    Product Catalog

    Why us?

    Our Products

    We do not skimp on good products in pursuit of cheapness, we do not "improve" our creations by adding preservatives, improvers, flavors, semi-finished products, powders and other chemicals.
    Despite the fact that the modern market is actively trying to force us to make a mess with the composition and recruit an inexpensive workforce, we successfully resist this onslaught in the face of fierce competition.

    The Main Man in the Confectionery Shop

    Of course, this is our brand chef, pastry chef.All desserts are invented and developed under her supervision. We don't steal recipes: our brand's wonder head is full of ideas for implementation.
    We can proudly say that we treat our children with our sweets without a twinge of conscience, because we know what it is made of, with what hands and in what conditions.

    Result-oriented work

    We don't produce a mass-produced product that everyone likes and is inexpensive, but we do offer really delicious desserts!
    Our desserts are the product of inspiration and long hours of experimentation in search of an impressive result.
